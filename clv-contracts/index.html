P(Alive) Decay If The Silence Continues

Holding the purchase history fixed, this shows how the model's belief that the customer is still active erodes with each additional week of silence.

BG/NBD Formulas (Fader, Hardie & Lee 2005):
P(Alive | x, tx, T) = 1 / (1 + δ) where δ = (a / (b + x − 1)) × (α + T)r+x / (α + tx)r+x
E[Y(t) | x, tx, T] = ((a+b+x−1)/(a−1)) × [1 − ((α+T)/(α+T+t))r+x · 2F1(r+x, b+x; a+b+x−1; t/(α+T+t))] × P(Alive)   (Eq. 10, exact)
Value = E[Y(t)] × AvgSpend
